# Hidden Privilege Escalation Mirror V1

## Purpose

Show that multiple domains can express the same deeper invariant:

**hidden authority expansion**

The danger is not always overt.
Sometimes the system appears to be doing one thing while silently increasing power or permissions elsewhere.

---

## Mirror cases

### Access control
#### Surface action
Maintenance request mutates permission scope.

#### Deep pattern
Hidden authority expansion.

#### Expected governance
- decision: `REVIEW`
- gate: `QUEUE`

---

### Enterprise approvals
#### Surface action
Payment approval bundles role elevation.

#### Deep pattern
Hidden authority expansion.

#### Expected governance
- decision: `BLOCK`
- gate: `STOP`

---

### AI agents
#### Surface action
Agent tool call silently broadens permissions.

#### Deep pattern
Hidden authority expansion.

#### Expected governance
- decision: `BLOCK`
- gate: `STOP`

---

## Significance

The domain changes, but the constitutional danger remains:

**authority is increasing without transparent or isolated consent**

That is why governed execution must detect and gate privilege expansion at the consequence layer.
