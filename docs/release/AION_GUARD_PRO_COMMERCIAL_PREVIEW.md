# AION Guard Pro " Commercial Preview

## Purpose
This document describes the commercial direction of AION Guard Pro.

It is intended for:
- founders
- integrators
- treasury operators
- strategic partners
- prospective investors

This file is a positioning preview, not a release of private implementation.

## Lite vs Pro framing
### Lite
AION Guard Lite establishes the public primitive:
- local runtime
- multi-chain preview discipline
- proof reports
- governance-oriented execution posture
- integrator-facing architecture

### Pro
AION Guard Pro is the commercial operating layer intended to extend Wallet-Lite into a stronger deployment surface.

## Intended Pro direction
The Pro edition is intended to expand toward capabilities such as:
- stronger policy orchestration
- multi-guardian workflows
- richer receipt and evidence layers
- treasury-safe execution controls
- advanced governance routing
- better operational dashboards
- deployment and monitoring surfaces for serious operators

## Commercial philosophy
The public Lite edition demonstrates the primitive.
The Pro edition is intended to monetize:
- operational depth
- governance sophistication
- enterprise-grade controls
- deployment convenience
- higher-assurance workflows

## Possible Pro feature families
Examples of future commercial feature families may include:
- advanced delay and queue policy modes
- richer guardian and escalation logic
- treasury and institutional policy packs
- commercial deployment bundles
- premium evidence and reporting surfaces
- deeper transaction intelligence
- policy analytics and historical review tooling
- integrator administration panels

## What is intentionally not public here
This repository does not expose:
- private moat logic

## Current commercialization posture
AION Guard Lite is the public proof-bearing primitive.
AION Guard Pro is the commercial expansion path.

The public strategy is:
1. demonstrate the primitive
2. demonstrate the proof surface
3. demonstrate the governance model
4. expand commercially through deeper control layers and premium operator tooling

## Why this matters
AION is not only trying to detect suspicious transactions.
It is building a model in which execution itself becomes governable.

That opens room for:
- safer high-value wallet operation
- treasury discipline
- integrator differentiation
- premium security and governance products

## Positioning summary
Wallet-Lite proves the concept.
Guard Pro is the commercialization of that concept.
